Types of Crypto Scam You Might Have Faced
Crypto scams come in many forms, from Ponzi schemes to phishing attacks. Learn about the different types you might have encountered and how to protect yourself.

Someone Scammed Your Cryptocurrency

You may have invested in a fake platform, fallen victim to catphishing, or clicked on a YouTube promotion promising huge returns. Perhaps you sent your crypto to an "influencer" or got caught in a pump-and-dump scheme?

Your Wallet Was Hacked

Your wallet might have been compromised through a phishing link, a device hack, or weak security. Hackers can quickly drain your funds once they gain access.

You Lost Access to Your Cryptocurrency

Have you lost your passphrase or device? Maybe you accidentally sent crypto to the wrong address? These situations can feel hopeless but aren’t always irreversible.
